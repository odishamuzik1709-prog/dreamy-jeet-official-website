About Dreamy Jeet

Independent Rapper • Producer • Founder of OdishaMuzik Records

Dreamy Jeet is an independent hip-hop artist from Odisha, India, known for blending Drill, Trap and Hip-Hop with Indian culture, real-life experiences and cinematic storytelling.

Starting as an independent creator, Dreamy Jeet built his own path through music production, songwriting and visual storytelling. His sound represents ambition, struggle, dreams and the voice of a new generation.

As the founder of OdishaMuzik Records, his vision is to create a platform for independent artists and introduce a unique sound called BharatTrap — a fusion of Indian identity with modern global Hip-Hop.

Every release represents originality, resilience and the journey of an artist building something from the ground up.
